Stranded I

Struggle for survival!

In the 3D adventure Stranded you slip into the role of a cast away, stranded on a desert island.

Your objective is simple: Survive! After ensuring this surival you have to care about a way back home. To achieve that you have to collect or even grow food, produce tools and build structures.

It becomes even harder when you spot wild animals. You have to equip yourself with the right weapons to defend your life, otherwise you might end up as lion food!

Besides the random map with an always different look there are three more islands with special missions. An included map editor allows you to create your own islands.

Features

  • big islands
  • a lot of animals and plants
  • many different items
  • 7 buildable buildings/vehicles
  • 10 weapons/tools
  • day-night cycle
  • map editor with definable win conditions

Version

Version: Final/Gold 1.0.3.0Release: 05.12.2003
